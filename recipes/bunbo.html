Bun Bo

Description

Here's a classic Vietnamese Bún Bò Huế recipe — a flavorful, spicy beef noodle soup from the city of Huế. It's not as sweet as phở and has a bold, lemongrass-infused broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shank or brisket
  • 500 g pork hock or pork leg (optional)
  • 3 stalks lemongrass (smashed and tied)
  • 1 large onion (charred)
  • 1 small piece of ginger (charred)

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth
  2. Season the broth
  3. Make chili oil
  4. Cook noodles
  5. Assemble bowls
